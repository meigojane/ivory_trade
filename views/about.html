ABOUT

IVORYFREE serves as a starting point for research and a platform where it gives basic understanding of ivory trade and poaching and gathers published articles and datasets surrounding the subject of ivory trade.

We are here to raise awareness and provide as much resources as possible for people who care or starting to care.

Tens of thousands of elephants are being killed across Africa each year for their tusks. A century ago, there were maybe 5 million elephants across Africa. Now there are less than 500,000.
